How the math works:
Flip Profit: ARV (after repair value from comps) − purchase price − rehab costs. ROI = profit ÷ total cost.
Est Rent: Based on area comps ($2-3/sqft in OC, $1.80-2.20 IE). Cross-referenced with Zillow rent estimates.
Rental Cash Flow: Rent − mortgage (7%, 80% LTV, 30yr) − property tax (1.2%) − insurance (~$200/mo). Negative = you lose money monthly.
Rehab: $35-50/sqft SoCal average for moderate renovation. Adjusted by property condition signals.
Note: Mobile homes excluded. All estimates — verify with your own inspections and comps.
